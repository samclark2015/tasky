# Settings

Open Settings by clicking the gear icon at the bottom of the sidebar.

---

## Appearance

### Theme

Choose how Tasky looks:

| Option | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Always use the light theme |
| Dark | Always use the dark theme |
| System | Follow the OS dark/light mode setting |

You can also change the theme from the **View → Theme** menu in the macOS menu bar.

---

## Sync

### Auto-sync Interval

Controls how often Tasky automatically syncs with connected accounts.

| Setting | Behavior |
|---|---|
| Off | Manual sync only |
| 5 min | Sync every 5 minutes |
| 15 min | Sync every 15 minutes |
| 30 min | Sync every 30 minutes |
| 60 min | Sync every hour |

### Sync Now

Click **Sync Now** to trigger an immediate sync of all accounts regardless of the interval setting.

---

## Accounts

Manage CalDAV and GitHub accounts that Tasky syncs with.

### Adding an Account

Click **Add Account** and choose **CalDAV** or **GitHub**. Follow the setup steps — see [Sync](sync.md) for detailed instructions.

### Editing an Account

Click the account row to expand it. Use the **Edit** button to update credentials or linked calendars/repos.

### Removing an Account

Click the account row to expand it, then click **Remove**. This deletes the account and all its linked calendar/repo mappings. Tasks that were synced via that account are kept locally.

### Linked Calendars and Repos

Each account row expands to show its linked calendars (CalDAV) or repositories (GitHub). You can link or unlink each one to a task list directly from the expanded row.
