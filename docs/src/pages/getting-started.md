# Getting Started with Tasky

Tasky is a native desktop task manager with built-in sync for CalDAV servers and GitHub Issues.

## Installation

Download the latest release for your platform from the [releases page](https://github.com/anomalyco/tasky/releases) and open the installer.

## First Launch

On first launch you'll see:

- **Sidebar** on the left — navigation (Today, Inbox, Calendar, Planner, Search), your task lists, and buttons for sync and settings at the bottom.
- **Main content area** — the currently active view.
- **Details panel** on the right — opens when you select a task.

The app starts in the **Today** view. Your task lists appear in the sidebar below the main navigation items.

## Creating Your First Task

There are three ways to create a task:

1. **Keyboard shortcut:** Press `N` from anywhere in the app to open the New Task modal.
2. **QuickAdd:** Type directly into the "Add a task..." input that appears at the bottom of most views and press `Enter`.
3. **Modal form:** Click the `+` icon or press `N` to open the full task creation form with all fields.

Once created, click any task to open its details in the panel on the right.
